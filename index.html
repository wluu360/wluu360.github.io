Meet Ichthyosaur

We know the dinosaurs that roamed the surface of the Earth,
but what creatures roamed the seas?

Ichthyosaurs were sea monsters that dominated the world ’s oceans and although resembling a dolphin, they were ancient reptilian predators. With
fish-like bodies, and long narrow skulls that came equipped with sharp teeth, they were able to hunt their prey and zip through water with great speed. With some of the largest ever recorded eyes and streamlined bodies, they were adapted to life in sea and came on top in the marine food chain.

Although the cause of their extinction is still a mystery to us today, it only means there is still so much to learn and so much more to discover about these creatures.

Come and explore with us as we uncover its time on earth, its morphology, diet, lifestyle and habitat; all the striking characteristics that truly make this creature, the Ichthyosaurs, one of the most exquisite creatures to have ever roamed the seas.







Fact:

Ichthyosaurus possessed thick, massive ear bones which likely conveyed subtle vibrations in the surrounding water to it 's inner ear; an adaptation for locating and eating fish as well as avoiding encroaching predators
